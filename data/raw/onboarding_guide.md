# Employee Onboarding Guide

## 1. First Day
On the first day, employees will:
- Receive company equipment
- Get access to internal systems
- Attend an onboarding session with HR

---

## 2. Probation Period
The standard probation period is **6 months**.

Performance will be reviewed after 3 months and at the end of the probation period.

---

## 3. Mandatory Training
New employees must complete:
- Information security training
- Code of conduct training
- Data protection training

All mandatory training must be completed within the first **30 days**.

---

## 4. Buddy Program
Each new employee is assigned a buddy to support onboarding and integration
during the first 90 days.
