# Benefits Overview

## 1. Health Insurance
The company provides private health insurance coverage for all full-time employees from day one.

Coverage includes:
- General medical care
- Specialist consultations
- Dental
- Vision
- Hospitalization

---

## 2. Pension Plan
The company contributes **5% of gross salary** to the employee’s pension plan.

Employees may make additional voluntary contributions.

---

## 3. Learning & Development
Each employee receives an annual **learning budget of €1,500**.

The budget may be used for:
- Online courses
- Conferences
- Professional certifications

Manager approval is required before purchase.
