# Remote Work Policy

## 1. Eligibility
Employees who have completed their probation period are eligible for remote work.

Remote work eligibility depends on role responsibilities and team coverage needs.

---

## 2. Allowed Remote Days
Employees may work remotely up to **2 days per week**.

Additional remote days require written approval from the department manager.

---

## 3. Equipment & Security
Employees are responsible for:
- Using company-approved devices
- Securing confidential information
- Reporting security incidents immediately

Public Wi-Fi may only be used with company VPN enabled.

---

## 4. Revocation
The company reserves the right to revoke remote work privileges at any time
due to performance, security, or business reasons.
