# HR Handbook

Welcome to the company! This handbook outlines our core values, policies, and expectations.

## 1. Employment Basics
All employees are expected to act professionally, comply with company policies,
and uphold company values.

Employment is at-will unless otherwise stated in a signed agreement.

---

## 2. Working Hours
Standard working hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00–17:30 local time.
Flexible working arrangements may be approved by the employee’s manager.

---

## 3. Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to **2 days per week**, subject to team needs
and manager approval.

Remote work must comply with data security and confidentiality requirements.

---

## 4. Code of Conduct
Employees must:
- Treat colleagues with respect
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Protect confidential company information

Violations may result in disciplinary action.

---

## 5. Disciplinary Process
Disciplinary actions may include:
- Verbal warning
- Written warning
- Termination of employment
