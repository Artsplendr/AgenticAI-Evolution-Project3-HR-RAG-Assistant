# Leave Policy

## 1. Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to **30 days of paid annual leave** per calendar year.

Leave requests must be submitted at least **2 weeks in advance** and approved
by the employeeâ€™s manager.

---

## 2. Sick Leave
Employees may take sick leave when unfit for work.

A medical certificate is required for absences longer than **3 consecutive days**.

---

## 3. Public Holidays
Employees are entitled to all public holidays applicable in their country
of employment.

---

## 4. Carry-Over
Up to **5 unused leave days** may be carried over to the next calendar year
and must be used by March 31.

---

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Full-time employees accrue 20 days of PTO per calendar year.
- PTO requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance when possible.

---

## Parental Leave
- Refer to regional policy and applicable laws.
