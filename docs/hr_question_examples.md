## Example HR Questions

	1.	How many days per week am I allowed to work remotely?
	2.	Do I need manager approval for remote work?
	3.	Can my remote work privileges be revoked? If so, why?
    4.	How many days of annual leave do employees receive per year?
	5.	How far in advance do I need to request annual leave?
	6.	Do I need a medical certificate if I am sick for several days?
	7.	Can I carry unused leave days into the next year?
    8.	What health insurance benefits are provided to employees?
	9.	Does the company contribute to a pension plan?
	10.	Is there a budget for training or professional development?
    11.	How long is the probation period for new employees?
	12.	What training must be completed during onboarding?
	13.	Is there a buddy assigned to new employees?
    14.	What happens if an employee violates the code of conduct?
	15.	Under what conditions can disciplinary action lead to termination?

For each, expected sources should map to sections in `hr_handbook.md`, `leave_policy.md`, or `benefits_overview.md`.
